Weekly Burger Review. 12-07-2011.

The Mexican!

Welcome to our first “Weekly Burger Review” which we do every Wednesday. Unlike Saturday where we feature a place and order the classic cheeseburger, we order a specialty burger at one place, and we have a-lot of awesome burgers in town!

Today, we went to the Arctic Roadrunner and ordered a burger called “The Mexican.” This burger goes for $6.25 and is very unique. The Mexican burger has green chili peppers and salsa and the salsa has a bit of a small kick. Here is your first look at The Mexican.

The Mexican features mayo, green chili, salsa, one pickle, lettuce, onion, & tomatoes (PLOT.) There is a topping bar to add more pickles, mustard, and more. Something I recommend is put the hot pickles and peppers in the burger as well, kick it up a notch. I recommend holding the burger with napkins because the salsa and mayo do drip out while holding the burger. I really enjoy the salsa and chili pepper along with the hot pickles I got from the topping bar. This along with a soda is a great treat for lunch.

Just be warned of two things. One, this place does get packed and very busy as it was on the day of review. Second, cash only here no credit cards, no checks, or Euros accepted.
