Arctic Roadrunner Anchorage, AK. 12-10-2011.

Since 1964 Arctic Roadrunner has been trading cash for burgers and has been considered one of the best burger stops in Anchorage. We’ll see what Arctic Roadrunner is about, the burgers that make this place what it is, and more. So get ready as we make you hungry and crave another burger. But bring cash, because it’s cash only at the Arctic Roadrunner.

I visited the Arctic Roadrunner location on Old Seward near Campbell Creek and the building is beautiful. When you walk in you see pictures everywhere of all the loyal customers that made Arctic Roadrunner what it is today. You’ll also find pictures of the journey Arctic Roadrunner experience as it has grown.

They’re two menus to scan to select what you want to order. The one behind the order counter which shows the price and burgers. The other menu located on the wall to your left, when you walk in, which has a description of what ingredients are used in each burger. As I mentioned this place is cash only. The prices are a bit more then the big four (McDonald, Burger King, Wendy’s, & Carl Jr’s/Hardees.)

I went ahead and ordered the “Arctic Cheese Burger,” which goes for $5.95 and along with a regular fries and soda it goes for around seven dollars. The sodas are Coke products and it’s a self serve fountain. Next to the fountain is the topping bar with pickles, onions, pepper, and the usual condiments. After a short wait my number was called and here is the Arctic Cheese Burger.

The Arctic Cheese looks impressive, it’s tall, and like all burgers here one pickle, so pickle fans should get some beforehand. The burger has the typical PLOT (Pickle, Lettuce, Onion, & Tomatoes) on the bottom rather than top and mayo. I strongly suggest holding the burger with napkins underneath because the mayo does drip out.

There is definitely some seasoning in the beef, it’s a pretty good size, beef is juicy and just taste great. The French Fries (which are unsalted) were another great part of the meal. The beef with the seasoning really stand and overall it is a great burger. Of course they have a huge selection of different burgers like the Mexican which was reviewed earlier, and many more.

Next week wee b right back with another cheeseburger and another place!
