Burger Express Anchorage, AK. 12-26-2011.

So you find yourself in the Dimond Center, shopping because either you need to buy stuff or cause you were dragged here. Let say you then get a craving for a burger, and the Wendy’s in the food court won’t cut it today. Well, you’re in luck because of Burger Express.

Burger Express located across from the Dimond Center Cinema has a large selection if burgers, breakfast food, and foot long hotdogs as well. They do have a daily special and today special was Bacon Cheeseburger. Once you walk in you’ll notice the traditional dinner feel with stool seating, and booths.

You order first just like any burger stop. Note refills here are not free, its $.75, so may want to get a medium drink and they serve Pepsi products. The menu is good price, and we will be back again here to try out another burger at some point.

So, ordered the cheeseburger, got the medium Pepsi, and after a while burger is here, and this burger was pretty good.

The burger has the usual PLOT, but underneath the burger patty, and the top has cheese and mayo. The fries are really good as well and overall, for the small amount to pay extra, compare to Wendy’s and Burger King, I give it good marks. On the table are squirt bottle of ketchup and mustard for you to add onto the burger.

Overall I enjoyed my time, and the place does get packed once in a while. So if you’re in Dimond Center, and want a good burger, try Burger Express. If the line is long, shop about 10-15mins and come back.
