Weekly Burger Review. 12-23-2011.

The Dallas & "W" Burger!

This week, we make a surprise two burger review! Not one but two! We first hit up Wendy’s and see what the “W” Burger is all about. We than hit up Jerry Doughnuts & Burgers and see what they got cooking. So welcome to another Weekly Burger Review where we highlight a burger and hope you try it out!

Our first stop is Wendy’s off Spenard road to try the “W” burger. Wendy’s has introduced this burger to really compete against the Whopper from Burger King, McDonald’s Big Mac, and Carl Jr’s Big Carl. Here is the “W” burger!

The burger contains the usual PLOT (Pickles, Lettuce, Onions, & Tomatoes) along with Thousand Island dressing similar to the Big Mac and Big Carl. The “W” features the nice red onions and ingredients seen on the premium menu, rather than the value menu. The first bite and I must say pretty good. This will for sure establish Wendy’s as a serious threat to Burger King. Wendy’s is projected to surpass Burger King by 2012. McDonald’s is well ahead of the two, and Carl Jr is a respectable 4th place.

So that is the W, and someday we will do a special comparing the Big Mac, Whopper, W, and Big Carl together in one room, with three judges, and see who we recommend.

Now for the real review, the Weekly Burger, and this week, we go off a tip of a fan! Jasmine suggests we try Jerry’s Doughnuts & Burgers off International Airport Road across from Taco Loco. So we made our way to Jerry’s.

So the burger we tried at Jerry’s is called the Dallas Burger. The Dallas Burger consist of the usual PLOT, but also peppers and ham. Here is the Dallas Burger!

The Dallas Burger! I really like the bun a lot lightly toasted, and the fries are unsalted. The ketchup is really good here with flavor and taste. It does have a kick the burger and I enjoyed it.

We’ll no doubt be back again to feature the place for a Burger Stop review. Trust me, they have 23 different burgers to choose from, we’ll be back.

So thank you for reading, and know any place with an awesome burger, let us know. Monday we take a break from all the shopping, and enjoy a cheeseburger.
