Weekly Burger Review. 12-29-2011.

The Guacamole Burger!

WMo's O’Brady Burger and Brew, located next to the Carr’s on Huffman Road is the site for our next burger. This place has been around a while and has a good reputation. This will be our first non-burger stop visit. For those new, Burger Stop is when you order, pay, and eat. This will be our first dine in restaurant review. We want to find where the best burgers are and we will go anywhere! So today it is O’Brady!.

O’Brady has a big selection of burgers to choose from and today, I feel like some guacamole and sprouts and therefore I ordered the Guacamole Burger!

The burger looks really good and wow is it loaded. The Guacamole Burger has the traditional PLOT, sprouts, and guacamole. Not only is the burger looking good, but so are the fries, which are bottomless! Yes, that’s right bottomless steak fries and fountain soda (which is Coke products)! Come here hunrgry, and leave happy.

The burger was really good. The guacamole was good and wasn’t too much to make a mess, but just enough to get the flavor in each bit. Along with the beef which I found great and the spouts this burger was a joy to eat. Even after a refill of fries and soda, I still managed to finish the burger, the fries, and soda.

If you like guacamole and sprouts, you have to try this burger. The sesame seed bun may give it a generic burger appearance at first. But trust me, one bite, and you know it’s no ordinary burger. So leave feedback on our Facebook or Twitter. If you agree with me or disagree, I’d like to know. Also, know any other places with creative burgers, let us know. We want to try it out.
