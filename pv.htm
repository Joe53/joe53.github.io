Pancho Villa Anchorage, AK 01-09-2011.

Welcome fans to another review and we hope the best for everyone in Cordova and Valdez as they dig out of all the snow. Today we once again feature a cheeseburger, but this time it won’t be at a place you think of for good burgers. Thanks to an Alaska Burger Blog fan for informing us that this place serves good burgers, so tip for everyone, find a good burger, let us know.

Today we visited Pancho Villa Restaurant On 31st and Spenard. Pancho Villa has been around for a really long time and is well known in the Spenard area. I really enjoyed the atmosphere inside the restaurant with the dim lights, the booths, and the bar too. On the menu, in the American Dishes section is where you will find the option for hamburger or cheeseburger.

Now, it is a bit odd ordering a cheeseburger when you can smell great Mexican food all around. I was tempted to change order, but then I wouldn’t have a review to post today. So I ordered the cheeseburger which goes for $7.95. There are only two burger options as I mention. The burgers comes with fries, and for drinks you have the option of Coke products, and a bar if over 21.

The cheeseburger arrives and it looks great. The burger comes with the usual PLOT and an interesting note. The burger comes with one big leafy lettuce, and shredded lettuce. Once the burger is assembled with everything, it is rather pretty tall, and wow does it look good. For sure wrapped the napkin around this burger, as the shredded lettuce may fall out underneath. First bite, and it was good.

The beef has some seasoning and the shredded lettuce really is an awesome treat. There is no mayo or mustard on this burger. With the French fries this does fill you up. The burger with all the toppings was great and interesting tip to, remember you have salsa, so if you want to kick it up a notch, put some of the hot salsa on the burger. Overall I was happy we made it here to Pancho Villa. Not the first place you think of when craving a burger, but if you're in a situation where someone wants a burger and others Mexican food, well there is Pancho Villa.

Feedback welcome and next week, new place, new burger. Till Next Time, Eat well my friends!
